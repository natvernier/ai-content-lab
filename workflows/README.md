# Workflows

This folder documents AI-supported workflows for content creation, review, approval and measurement.

The focus is on practical workflow design: who does what, when human review is needed, where risk appears and how learning signals are captured.

## Possible contents

- human-in-the-loop workflows
- content review flows
- approval processes
- escalation paths
- quality gates
- measurement loops

## Working principle

AI adoption becomes real when workflows change.

A workflow should make roles, decisions, risks and quality checks visible.
